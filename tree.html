TREE OF LIFE MISSIONS

Co-founders: Missionary Patricia Hamburg, Somerset Bible Baptist Church, New Jersey, USA, and Pastor Justin Nenge, Assistant Pastor at Truth Baptist Church, Nyanya, Abuja FCT, Nigeria.

USA Missionary Patricia Hamburg

She has been serving in Nigeria for almost 20 years. Nine of those years CALHAM International Women's Resource Centre, teaching woman Bible, sewing health and childbirth preparation. She was instrumental in starting lighthouse independent Bible Baptist Church, in Ibadan. A Co-founder of Tree of life Missions, a ministry of Truth Baptist Church Abuja that reached hundreds of souls. She is privileged to travel across Nigeria, Speaking at churches and in remote Villages reaching thousands of Nigerians with the Gospel and has seen many lives changed. She also teaches English at Providence Baptist Collage Seminary in Nyanya, Abuja.

Rev. Justin Mathias Nenge

Is the assistant pastor of Truth Baptist Church, Nyanya, and Abuja. He is a living testimony of God's grace. A graduate of providence Baptist College and Seminary, Eligin, Illinois, USA, he was licensed and ordained in 2011. He was instrumental in starting several local churches in Nigeria. He has travelled alongside USA missionaries across the country with the Gospel. Rev. Justin is the co-founder/director of Tree of Life Missions, with the aim of reaching remote rural villages with the Gospel. Additionally, he is the founder of Bible proclaimers Student Fellowship reaching the students with the Gospel and educating them with Bible moral Rev. Justin is privileged to travel alongside American preachers across Nigeria villages. He reached thousands with the Gospel and had seen many lives changed. He is married to Grace and where blessed with children.

Rev. Missionary Isreal Thompsons

Is a local church missionary, a Nigerian national, who has served both inside and outside of Nigeria.He was instrumental in establishing about 20 local churches. Churches he has served and founded as missionary include Delta independent Baptist church,Effirun, Warri, Delta State, Spring Valley Baptist Church Kubwa, and spring Valley Baptist Church Tunga Maje, both in Abuja. He is the founder/director of in Trust With The Gospel publication and Baptist Heritage Children and Aged Harbour, Zuba-Gwagwalada Expressway, Tunga Maje, Abuja. He is ready preacher and has written and published many books. He is married to Sarah.

Rev. Dr. Mark Joseph Holmes

A USA missionary serving in Nigeria for 15 years, has been instrumental to the establishment of 40 local churches throughout Nigeria. He is the founder of Truth Baptist Church, Nyanya Abuja, and Grace and Glory Baptist Church, Jahi, Abuja. Providence Baptist College and Seminary and Temple Baptist Institute were also founded and directed by Missionary Holmes. He is the African Director of Student Mission Institute for Training in Evangelism (SMITE) youth camps in three countires Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana and the Soul-Winning and Leadership Conferences (Pastor' seminars in six locations). A ready preacher,he has travelled all across the country of Nigeria Rev. Holmes is married to Sabrina and blessed with children.

This Ministry

In 2015 to meet our personal Soul-Winning duties as Christians. Pastor Justin was recovering from his accident, resulting in loss of his left leg and Missionary Patricia, because of her age and recovering from knee surgery was no longer able to walk the streets sharing the Gospel. Since that time we see the Lord working all across Nigeria. We have ministered in Makurdi, Vandeikyia, Ugba, Ogoja, IDP camps and others places in Benue State; Jos, Zarazon, Shendam, Panchine and Langtang in Plateau State; Minna in Niger State; Calabar in Rivers State; Ogbomoso, and Ibadan in Oyo State ; and to Port Harcourt; and to Osun State; Churches in Kaduna and in Katsina, churches in Port Harcourt and Uyo in Akwa Ibom; Fulani village in Keana LGA in Nasarawa State and soon in Kwara State. So many places that I have lost track of them all. We have taught and preached to small groups and to groups of thousands. We have taught and preached in the daytime and in the nighttime using torchlight. And God has moved in mighty ways every time!! We bring the Gospel to schools, villages, even roadside areas where people are gathered. We encourage other independent Baptist churches doing revival meetings, ladies conferences, & marriage seminars for couples. Wherever God has led us, we have gone. As a team we travel with a driver and an interpreter as needed. Occasionally, a Seminary student will travel with us to learn about the different field opportunities. The ministry started out at our local level and soon requests for meetings came to us from across Nigeria. People are hungry for the Gospel. The Gospel still works, and God is still saving souls.!!!! We average about 8 meetings a year We have seen miracles upon miracles as we travel. Our village visits are especially rewarding as we listen to testimonies over the years of how God has changed lives and restored marriages. We travel by faith and go to some of the most dangerous places in the world. God always takes care of us and protects us. We have seen people who were sick and received Christ only to learn that shortly after they passed onto eternity. We've been to villages where they beg us to stay and start churches because they want their children to grow up in the truth. The fields are truly white unto harvest, but the laborers are few. We've seen marriages restored and lives turned around. So many testimonies of God's grace and goodness. We often show Christian films in the villages and see great responses in Fulani and Muslim villages. Even in the rain, hundreds turn out for these shows and receive Christ. There is still much work to be done. There is a cost, and many sacrifices to be made. But God is glorified, and we will see the rewards in Heaven one day. A great multitude of souls saved. Please pray for Missionary Patricia and Pastor Justin for good health, continued wisdom and strength, as we continue in this great ministry.
