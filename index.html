What Are We Doing?

At city of Refuge we provide a safe haven for displaced and persecuted Fulani Christians. We seek to educate them in the Bible and encourage them to be self-reliant. We encourage them to understand that Jesus and Salvation is the key to happiness and holiness in their daily life. A property was purchased in a wide-open area(a safe haven). We built one unit of eight double rooms and a passage that will provide housing for families. Another unit of single rooms was built for those who are single. Access to a two-toilet water system is also available for a clean water supply. We provide temporary housing, clothing, and feeding for them. They are able to worship without fear, learn skills to rebuild their lives and provide for themselves, educate their children and become independent ultimately, they will be empowered to evangelize their own public ethnic groups with the Gospel.
